# Customer_Analytics_Dashboard
### Power BI · Customer Analytics · Workforce Intelligence

Three end-to-end business analytics dashboards built on real operational 
data — analyzing call centre performance, customer retention risk, 
and workforce diversity across a global organization.

---

## Dashboards

### 1. Call Centre Performance Analysis
**Business Question:** Which agents are underperforming and when is call 
volume highest?

**Key Metrics:**
- 5,000 total calls analyzed across January — March
- 4,054 answered (81%) | 946 unanswered (19%)
- Average satisfaction rating: 3.40 / 5.0
- Average speed of answer: 67.52 seconds

**Key Findings:**
- Peak call volume occurs between 11:00 AM and 1:00 PM daily
- Martha has the highest satisfaction rating (3.47) 
  while Joe has the lowest (3.33)
- Joe has the slowest answer speed at 70.99 seconds — 
  5% above the team average
- Streaming and payment-related issues account for 41% of all calls

---

### 2. Customer Retention Analysis
**Business Question:** Which customers are most at risk of churning 
and what drives churn?

**Key Metrics:**
- 7,043 total customers
- 26.54% churn rate — 1,869 customers lost
- $16.06M total revenue | $64.76 average monthly charge
- Average customer tenure: 32.37 months

**Key Findings:**
- Fiber optic internet customers (43.96%) have the highest churn risk
- Electronic check payment method correlates with highest churn
- Customers without online security or tech support churn at nearly 
  double the rate of those with these services
- Long tenure customers (60+ months) generate the most total revenue 
  — retention of this segment is critical

---

### 3. HR Workforce Diversity Analysis
**Business Question:** Is the organization achieving gender balance 
across departments and seniority levels?

**Key Metrics:**
- 500 total employees
- 295 male (59%) | 205 female (41%)
- 93% full time | 7% part time
- Operations is the largest department with 203 employees

**Key Findings:**
- Gender imbalance is most pronounced at senior levels — 
  executive and director roles skew heavily male
- Switzerland and Europe account for 80%+ of total headcount
- Junior Officer level has the largest hiring gap (102 target hires needed)
- FY20 leaver rate is highest in Operations and Sales & Marketing
